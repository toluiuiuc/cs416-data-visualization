How is the house market?

Zillow House Value Index

Price appreciation, according to the Zillow Home Value Index (ZHVI)[1], finally turned the corner after setting new record highs for 13 straight months, as its annual growth rate dipped from 20.9% in April to 19.8% in Jun.
The typical U.S. home is now worth $354,165 — nearly $60,000 more than last year and almost $95,000 higher than in May 2020.
